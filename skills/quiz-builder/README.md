# Quiz Builder

Generates scenario-based question banks that discriminate: one defensible answer, distractors that are wrong for diagnosable reasons, honest difficulty labels, in a format that imports into your LMS.

Use it to build or expand a question bank in any subject. Domain-selectable, and built to add more domains as you teach them.

## What's in this folder

- `SKILL.md`: the bank format, the prompt, choosing or combining domain packs and company sets, what to check, and the guardrail. Open this to run the skill.
- `domains/`: fifteen subject packs (business, marketing, analytics, and more) so questions feel native to your field on the first try. See `domains/README.md` for the full catalog and how to add your own.
- `companies.md`: a catalog of real companies grouped into sets, for scenario flavor.

Validate a generated bank with the exam-rebalance skill's difficulty and persona checks before it counts. To pull objectives from your real materials instead of pasting them, see `../prof-brain/` and `../../guides/canvas-lms.md` or `../../guides/other-lms.md`.
